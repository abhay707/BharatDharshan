Every state is a universe — a distinct language, cuisine, art form, and way of life.

The land of five rivers — wheat fields, the Golden Temple, and a culture of generosity that has fed armies and pilgrims alike.

35M Population

God's Own Country — backwater houseboats, Ayurveda, the spice coast, and the Kathakali stage where myth still walks at night.

81M Population

Land of kings and the Thar — pink cities, blue cities, golden cities, and forts that have looked out over the desert for a thousand years.

ChennaiTamil
77M Population

Temple country — Dravidian gopurams, Bharatanatyam, Carnatic music, and the unbroken Tamil tongue, oldest of the world's living classical languages.

KolkataBengali
99M Population

Land of the Hooghly and the howling tiger — Tagore's verses, Durga's autumn pandals, and the sweet white machinery of rosogolla.

The heart of India — Khajuraho's carved stone, Sanchi's stupa, Bhimbetka's painted shelters, and tigers that still walk the sal forests.
